Play:
My former professor and first spiritual teacher, Kirk, is working a book on about the spirit and importance of play. I have yet to full flesh out what play means to me, but I have more respect for it now than I did through my teen years. I try to carry the buoyancy and sincerity of play with me into everything that I do.
Kirk gave me a very heavy old penny, which is the coin I flip when I need guidance.
by Cornelia O'Donovan
