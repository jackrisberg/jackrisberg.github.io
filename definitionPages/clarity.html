Clarity (quality):
To exist in clarity is not easy. It means…
—sacrificing the euphoric haze of juggling uppers and downers
—sitting in the discomfort of boredom, and occasionally painful emotion
—not closing your eyes to the ugly things
—making time to revel in the present, to say “this is a day of my life.”
—recognizing and minimizing distraction
I live in a city and on the internet and these places are not conducive to clarity.
